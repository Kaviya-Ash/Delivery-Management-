About Us

Elite Food Packaging, established in 2017 by Honourable Ash Ketchum, is a pioneer in innovative and sustainable food packaging solutions. We are dedicated to creating eco-friendly packaging that not only protects food but also minimizes environmental impact. By using biodegradable, recyclable, and compostable materials, we ensure that our products contribute to a greener future.

Our Mission & Vision

Mission: To revolutionize the packaging industry by providing sustainable, high-quality solutions that meet business needs while protecting our planet.

Vision: To be the leading provider of eco-friendly packaging solutions worldwide, driving the transition towards a circular economy.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Are your products completely biodegradable?
A: Yes, all our products are made from 100% biodegradable and compostable materials that break down naturally without harming the environment.
Q: How long does it take for your packaging to decompose?
A: Our packaging decomposes within 3-6 months under proper composting conditions, compared to hundreds of years for conventional plastics.
Q: Do you offer bulk discounts?
A: Yes, we offer significant discounts for bulk orders. Contact us for custom pricing on large quantities.
Q: Are your products food-safe?
A: Absolutely! All our packaging products are FDA-approved and made from food-grade materials that are completely safe for food contact.
Q: Can I get custom branding on the packaging?
A: Yes, we offer custom printing services. Minimum order quantities apply for custom branding.
Q: What materials do you use for your products?
A: We use plant-based plastics, recycled paper, bamboo, sugarcane bagasse, and other sustainable materials.
Q: What is your minimum order quantity?
A: Our MOQ varies by product. Generally, it's 100 units for standard products and 500 units for custom branded items.
Q: How can I track my order?
A: Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number via email and SMS.
Q: What payment methods do you accept?
A: We accept online payments (credit/debit cards, UPI, net banking) and cash on delivery.
Q: What is your delivery time?
A: Standard delivery takes 3-5 business days. Express delivery options are available for urgent requirements.
Q: Do you ship internationally?
A: Currently, we ship across India. International shipping is available for bulk orders - please contact us for details.
Q: What is your return policy?
A: We accept returns within 7 days of delivery for damaged or defective products.
