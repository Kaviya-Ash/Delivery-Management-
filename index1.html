About Us

Elite Food Packaging, established in 2017 by Honourable Ash Ketchum, is a pioneer in innovative and sustainable food packaging solutions. We are dedicated to creating eco-friendly packaging that not only protects food but also minimizes environmental impact. By using biodegradable, recyclable, and compostable materials, we ensure that our products contribute to a greener future. Our commitment to sustainability drives us to continuously innovate, developing cutting-edge packaging solutions that meet the evolving needs of businesses while reducing plastic waste. At Elite Food Packaging, we believe that sustainability and functionality go hand in hand, making eco-conscious choices accessible without compromising on quality. At Elite Food Packaging, our motto—"Everything" - reflects our commitment to providing everything you need when it comes to food packaging. Whether it’s quality, sustainability, customization, or reliability, we ensure that our packaging solutions cover every aspect of your business’s needs
